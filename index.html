Biography

Brandon Paak Anderson, known professionally as Anderson Paak, is an American singer, rapper, songwriter, record producer and multi-instrumentalist from Oxnard, California. He was born on Febuary 8th, 1986. His genre of music is considered to be hip hop R&B groove soul. He released his debut mixtape, O.B.E. Vol. 1 in 2012. Since his debut, he has been nominated for five Grammy Awards.
